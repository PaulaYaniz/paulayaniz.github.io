About Me

Product-minded builder with experience in UX research, data-driven iteration, and cross-functional execution.

Hi, I’m Paula.

I study Computer Science and Business Administration at Washington and Lee University, and I focus on building products that are useful, intuitive, and grounded in real user needs.

My work spans geospatial analytics, digital platforms, user-facing tools, and operational problem-solving. I approach product work with three principles:

  • User Clarity: Understand real behavior through interviews, observation, and data.
  • Lean Execution: Ship small, validate early, iterate fast.
  • Impact Orientation: Focus on outcomes, not features.

I’ve led projects from research to launch across nonprofits, student groups, and startup environments. I enjoy solving messy problems, mapping ambiguity, and turning insights into actionable product direction.

Skills

  • Product research, roadmapping, MVP definition
  • Data analysis (Python, ArcGIS, SQL)
  • UX testing, user flows, rapid prototyping
  • Cross-functional coordination & stakeholder communication
